What factors do most impact on the result of Happiness Score?

Happiness is subjective and so calculating it can be complex. Researchers have explored various possible methods to calculate patterns of happiness. The aim of the report,is to provide another tool for governments, businesses and civil societies to help their countries find a better way to well-being.

In this research, I try to answer " What factors do most impact on the result of Happiness Score?" The measure is based on real GDP per capita, social support, healthy life expectancy and people’s perception of their freedom to make life choices, generosity, and perceptions of corruption. After the finding, GDP per capita and life expectancy at birth play the most important part.
